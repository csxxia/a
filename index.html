Biography: Dr. Xianjin XIA is currently a Research Assistant Professor (RAP) at the Department of Computing (COMP) of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HK PolyU). Before that, he joined PolyU as a Post-doctoral Fellow in Aug 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Yuanqing ZHENG. He received B.S. degree in Software Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China in 2010. He received M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the same university in 2013 and 2018, respectively. He is a member of IEEE and ACM. Know more about Xianjin on his official homepage.
